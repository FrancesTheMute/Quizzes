My Amazing Summer Holiday

Last summer, my family and I travelled to Spain for two weeks. We flew from Hamburg airport early in the morning and arrived in Barcelona at noon. The weather was fantastic – sunny and very hot every day!

We stayed in a small hotel near the beach. Every morning, I woke up early and went for a swim in the sea. The water was warm and crystal clear. My little brother, Finn, built sandcastles on the beach while my parents read books under a big umbrella.

In the afternoons, we often visited the city centre. Barcelona has incredible buildings, especially the famous church called the Sagrada Família. We also tried lots of local food. My favourite was paella, a rice dish with seafood and vegetables.

On our last evening, we watched the sun go down over the sea. It was the most beautiful thing I have ever seen. I can't wait to go back next summer!
